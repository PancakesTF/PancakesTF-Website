Community Rules & Guidelines

Hey there! Glad to see you reading the rules.
Remember to use the !calladmin command to report a disruptive player in game.

These rules apply to anyone in any House of Pancakes site/server, without exceptions to staff and donators.
Depending on the severity of the infraction, violators can and will be gagged, muted, kicked, and/or banned.

Gameplay & Sportsmanship

This is an 18+ community. Although this isn't strictly an adult community, you may run into adult situations.

Squeakers are tolerated until they turn on Call of Duty mode and start raging in mic or chat.

Adult Sprays are tolerated within reason.

If they are deemed too lewd, the player will be asked to remove it. If not, further punishments may be invoked.

Do not spam in voice/text chat or sounds.

This includes music, griefing, trolling, begging, advertising, harassment, saysounds, and defective game sounds.

Sticky camping, spawn camping, and related camping is somewhat tolerated within reason.

Allow the enemy team to safely leave spawn before engaging in a duel. An exception to this is when the enemy is engaging from spawn.

Harassing and getting revenge against players or staff outside of the game itself is prohibited.

We will not tolerate petty and childish attempts to harass players and admins in real life.

Cheats & Exploits

Abuse of exploits in server plugins, maps, modifications, and original game features are not tolerated.

Exploited features may also be removed, ruining the gaming experience for all.

Cheaters on the server are not tolerated and are, to some extent, automatically detected by the server and banned.

If you're a shitty enough player to use cheats, expect the banhammer to swing hard.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Admins/Mods are abusing me! Players are cheating/spamming/abusing! Abuse! Abuse!

Report any issues to staff personnel, discussions, Discord Server, or by typing !calladmin in game.

People won't stop killing me! I just want to taunt/dance/idle!

As a Steam Group Member in our Idle/Trade Server, type !friendly or !f to become invincible without interfering with anyone's gameplay.

How can I access the server features, if it has any?

You must be a Steam Group member to unlock server features. Unless stated otherwise, typing !menu will bring up the player menu.

I've been mistakenly punished. How can I appeal my punishment?

Go to our appellate forum and follow the instructions in the pinned topic.

How do I become a Moderator? How do I become an Admin?

We will take applications for new staff members when we've announced about it in our Steam Group.

My problem couldn't be solved here, what should I do?

Please share your inquiries with us in our Group Discussions or Discord Server.
